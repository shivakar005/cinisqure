Your digital E-Pad

Create personalised prescriptions just the way you like it, with a simple command.

Simple, fast and easy

With E-Pad you can create prescriptions with a simple command giving access to well-curated medical data. You have the flexibility to write short or detailed prescriptions, medical certificates, vaccine cards, and more.

Abracadabra 2

Understand your transactions before you send them and receive warnings about potentially harmful actions. Get full protection over all of your assets, with help from advanced simulations and suggested actions.

An EMR that is completely Customizeable

Easily create and customise prescription formats based on diseases, symptoms, encounter type and more, so that your clinical work has more usability and everything stays organised, your way.

Brand & design your prescriptions

Whether you are sharing prescriptions via SMS link, PDF or simple print, E-pad lets you completely brand your prescriptions with your workspace digital letterhead, logo watermarks and more.

Get started with over 100 Templates

Templates are the quickest and easiest way to kickstart your e-pad. Download from 100+ prescriptions categorised as per disease & speciality curated by best doctors. You can customise templates as per your need.
